Black Does Not Go With Navy Blue.

Why do people wear the two together?

Posted by Ashley Tripp on April 26, 2025

About ten years ago, I had to bear through the terrible decisions that my friend made. I didn't even know it was possible to make such a poor decision. This dear friend of mine loved to wear navy suits with black shoes. Now, please, don't misunderstand me. Blue suits go just fine with black shoes, though I think they go better with brown shoes, but navy suits and black shoes just cannot be spoken in the same sentence. 

I truly believe that this is not limited to suits. Although, I will be as gracious as I possibly can - it really does depend on the shade of blue. My friend thinks I am mad for having such a silly perspective, but I pity him truly. Perhaps one day, he will change his mind for the better.
