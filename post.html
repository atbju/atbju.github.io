Potato Chips Are Better Crushed Up. Change My Mind.

There is something that tastes entirely different in a bag of crushed up chips.

Posted by Ashley Tripp on April 26, 2025

For 14 years of my life, I thought I knew everything I needed to about potato chips. It wasn't until one morning that I found my bag of chips crushed at the bottom of my lunchbox that my life changed forever. At first, I was hesitant to eat them because there's something special about the crunch that each whole unique chip has. Nevertheless, I was too hungry to discard the bag. 

At that moment, I tasted chips in a way that I never had before. I could taste every flavor of the chip compacted into one tiny mouthful. While I no longer had the full crunch, it was still crunchy. I'm not sure I even know how to explain it - it's just something every person has to experience for themselves. 

You would not believe how often I get harassed for such a decision. Yes, the bag of chips does look significantly smaller when crushed to small bits and pieces, but it is worth it. I can't make this decision for anyone. You just have to trust me that it is a life-changing experience.
